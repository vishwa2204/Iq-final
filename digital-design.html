What is digital design?

It’s pretty simple to define digital design: it’s any form of design work done digitally for to be interacted. Think websites, apps or even social media. There are many number of different digital devices these days and a large amount of consideration is required to achieve best results in digital design. Digital
design process

Every brand is unique, so we tailor our digital design packages to fit your business. Whether you’re looking for a website, online store, social media graphics or more in-depth monthly support, we’re ready to help you.

01
Onboarding

The first stage is where we lay the foundation for a successful project. We outline the scope of digital work in a contract that we both agree. Then we’ll get together for a discovery meeting either on Zoom or in-person to learn about your goals and objectives.

02
Strategy / UX

Once we’re underway, we dive into detailed audience and competition research for your brand. We also look into trends and real world examples to make sure your brand is future proof. We present a strategy plan that includes Wireframes, Tone of voice, and visual elements for you to sign off

03
Design / UI

We then move into design stage. For websites, we present upto 3 homepage design options for you to choose from. Once you’re happy with the home page, we move on to the rest of the pages.

For social media, we provide a plan for the first month that includes post design, copy, and hashtags.

04
Development

Our well equipped team of developers can transform the design into fully integrated and mobile ready website.

05
Review & Publishing

Once you’re ready, the websites and social media posts are scheduled to be published. We carefully choose the content and publishing schedule in order to maximise the exposure.

06
Measure

We provide performance evaluation to see how your digital platforms are performing. We support ongoing plans to track and measure engagement and conversion.
